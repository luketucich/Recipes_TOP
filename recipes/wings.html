How To Make Buffalo Wings

Description

Buffalo wings are crispy chicken wings deep-fried and coated in a tangy, spicy sauce typically made from hot sauce, butter, and seasonings.

Originating in Buffalo, New York, in the 1960s, they're served hot and crispy, with the sauce ranging from mildly spicy to mouth-searing.

Often accompanied by celery sticks and blue cheese or ranch dressing, they're a beloved appetizer enjoyed at sports bars, parties, and gatherings

worldwide for their irresistible combination of crispy texture, old flavors, and fiery kick.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour (for dredging)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Optional: Celery sticks and blue cheese or ranch dressing for serving
